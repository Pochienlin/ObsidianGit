## Rules

$$log_{a}xy=log_ax+log_ay$$
$$log_a\frac{x}{y}=log_ax-log_ay$$
$$log_ax^n=nlog_ax$$
$$log_ab=\frac{log_cb}{log_ca}$$
$$log_ab=\frac{1}{log_ba}$$

### Further derived properties
$$log_a1=0$$
$$log_aa=1$$
$$log_aa^r=r$$
$$log_a\frac{1}{b}=-log_ab$$
$$log_\frac{1}{a}b=-log_ab$$
